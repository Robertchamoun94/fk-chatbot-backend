      Visiting a service centre

      The case administrators at Sweden’s service centres (servicecenter in Swedish) can help with your application, print out certificates and give you an update on your case. You can also submit applications and documents to Försäkringskassan at a service office.

      Kindly note that it is not possible to send registered mail to a service office. Send mail addressed to Försäkringskassan:
      Försäkringskassans Inläsningscentral
      839 88 Östersund

      The state's service center works on behalf of several authorities, so you can get help in several authority matters in one and the same place. In the list below are the addresses and opening hours to all the centres.
