If you have sought asylum in Sweden
Here, you can read about what Försäkringskassan is and what applies for asylum seekers in Sweden.
What is Försäkringskassan?
Försäkringskassan is a government agency that pays out money to families with children, as well as individuals who are sick or have a disability.
What am I entitled to from Försäkringskassan?
You are not entitled to any money from Försäkringskassan while you are waiting to be granted a residence permit. But, once you have been granted a residence permit, you may be entitled to money from Försäkringskassan.
