If you are injured during military training or while serving in civil defence
Annuity – compensation for lost work income due to personal injury
If you earn less money because you had to, for example, change work tasks or work fewer hours due to your personal injury.
Compensation for costs from a personal injury
If you have costs for dental care, special assistive devices or medical care abroad due to your personal injury.
Sickness benefit for personal injury during military training or while serving in civil defence
If you have an occupational injury but cannot receive sickness benefit or if your sickness benefit is low, you can apply for occupational injury sickness benefit.
