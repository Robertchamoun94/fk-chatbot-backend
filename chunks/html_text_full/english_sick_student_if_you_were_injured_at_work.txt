If you were injured at work
Annuity – compensation for lost work income due to occupational injury
If you earn less money because you had to, for example, change work tasks or work fewer hours due to an occupational injury.
Compensation for costs from an occupational injury
If you have costs for dental care, special assistive devices or medical care abroad due to your occupational injury.
Occupational injury sickness benefit
If you have an occupational injury but cannot receive sickness benefit or if your sickness benefit is low, you can apply for occupational injury sickness benefit.
