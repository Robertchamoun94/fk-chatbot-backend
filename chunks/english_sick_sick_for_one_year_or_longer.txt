Sick for one year or longer
Activity compensation in the event of sickness
If you are younger than 30 and have been sick at least one year.
Sickness compensation in the event of sickness
If you have a chronic illness and are between the ages of 30 and 64.
Housing supplement
If you receive sickness compensation or activity compensation, you may be entitled to housing supplement.
