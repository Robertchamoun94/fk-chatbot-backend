Alternative formats for people with disabilities
All humans are different with different abilities. If you have a disability that prevents you from reading what we write, you can request information in an alternative format that you can more easily access.
Talk to your administrator and tell us how you want the information, and we'll try to help you get it in an alternative format.
For example, you may need forms, documents or other information
uploaded as Daisy CD
braille
as sign language video
in large print.
You can also call us on 
0771‑524 524
.
