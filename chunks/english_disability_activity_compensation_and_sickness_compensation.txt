Activity compensation and sickness compensation
Activity compensation
Apply for activity compensation if you are younger than 30 and have been sick at least 1 year.
Activity compensation for extended schooling
Apply for activity compensation if you need more time to complete your schooling due to a disability.
Sickness compensation
Apply for sickness compensation if you have a chronic illness and are between the ages of 19 and 64.
Housing supplement
Apply for housing supplement if you are already receiving sickness compensation or activity compensation.
